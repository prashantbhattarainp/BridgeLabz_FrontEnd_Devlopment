Welcome to Kathmandu

Nestled in the Kathmandu Valley, the capital of Nepal blends ancient heritage with modern vibrancy. It’s a place where golden temples meet bustling markets, and every street whispers centuries of history.

Culture & Traditions

Kathmandu’s cultural rhythm beats through its festivals, art, and everyday life. From the colorful Holi to the sacred Indra Jatra, every celebration reflects the city’s deep spiritual roots and communal warmth.

Famous Landmarks

Must-visit sites include Swayambhunath Stupa, the serene Boudhanath, and the historic Durbar Square — each capturing the essence of Nepal’s architectural and religious brilliance.

Travel Tips

  • Best time to visit: October – December (clear skies, pleasant weather).
  • Try local dishes like Momo and Newari Khaja.
  • Respect temples — remove shoes and dress modestly.
